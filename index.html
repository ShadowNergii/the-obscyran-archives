Archive Dispatch 001

The Archives Open

Today marks the opening of The Obscyran Archives, the official public record for the world of Obscyra and the continuing development of PokéCyrus Online.

The first known region of Obscyra is Aetherra, a land shaped by forgotten histories, hidden relics, and mysteries that reward exploration, memory, and community discovery.

The Archives will preserve development updates, lore records, milestone reports, and future dispatches as the world continues to take form.

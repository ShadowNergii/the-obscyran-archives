Archive Dispatch — Haydr

Operation: Karel Reborn

Travelers. I am Haydr, keeper of these Archives. For a long while the roads of Karel lay quiet — but the first continent of Obscyra is stirring again.

Operation: Karel Reborn is the call to awaken it. Its coasts, its old paths, and the places where stories begin are being made ready for the first explorers to set foot upon them.

When you arrive, walk far and look closely. What you discover, the Archives will remember. This is only the first of many dispatches — keep watch for what comes next.
